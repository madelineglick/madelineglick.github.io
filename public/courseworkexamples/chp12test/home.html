About

Madeline Glick, Glick Art's artist, is a self taught commission artist who provides customers with personalized, quality art pieces. Glick has successfully worked with many customers and continues to, every day!

Mission

The main goal at Glick Arts is helping customers artistically express themselves. Any look, style, or idea can be created! With our accuracy and creativity, your piece will be nothing less than perfect!

Skills

Madeline's current medium experience includes (but is not limited to) acrylic paints, oil paints, watercolor paints, alcohol markers, oil pastels, digital softwares, clay, polymer clay, and glass work.
