20th Annual St. Clair County Law Day Race

The Law Day Run proceeds benefit the Land of Lincoln Legal Assistance Foundation, Inc.,
and other legal aid organizations, who provide low income and senior residents of southern and central
Illinois with high quality civil litigation services to obtain and maintain their basic human needs.
